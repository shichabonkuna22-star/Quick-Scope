About QuickScope

South Africa's trusted local service marketplace

Our Mission

QuickScope was born to simplify the way South Africans find and book local services. We believe in empowering local communities by connecting skilled providers with customers who need them. Our platform is designed to be transparent, secure, and easy to use – because getting help should never be complicated.

How It Works

1

Create an Account

Sign up as a customer to find services, or as a provider to list your offerings. It's free and takes only a minute.

2

Browse or List Services

Customers can search for services by category, location, or keywords. Providers can create detailed listings with photos and pricing.

3

Request a Scope or Book

For fixed-price services, book instantly. For custom jobs, request a scope – the provider will give you a quote.

4

Manage Bookings

Track your bookings, communicate with providers, and receive notifications when the job is confirmed or completed.

5

Review & Feedback

After a job is completed, leave a review to help others make informed decisions and to reward great service.

Why Choose QuickScope?

Verified & Trusted

All providers are vetted through customer reviews. You can see ratings before booking.

WhatsApp Integration

Connect with providers instantly via WhatsApp – no need to switch apps.

Transparent Pricing

See estimated costs upfront or request custom quotes. No hidden fees.

Local Focus

We prioritize South African providers and customers, supporting local economies.

Our Commitment to Safety

We take your safety seriously. All communications stay within the platform until you choose to connect via WhatsApp. We encourage users to verify identities and report any suspicious activity. Our review system helps maintain accountability.
